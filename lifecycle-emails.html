Lifecycle Emails

DocRaptor Customer Lifecycle Emails

copywriting, customer success research

These emails are part of DocRaptor's customer lifecycle campaign, and are sent at various key touch points during the customer's experience with the product: the first they successfully create a document, or if they haven't created a document within 24 hours of signing up.

Researching customer behavior revealed that trialing users who created a successful document within 24 hours were far more likely to continue generating documents, and remain happy, satisfied, and paying customers.

A follow up email is also sent upon user cancellation, and has proven to be a fantastic source for identifying weak points of the service, inform the development team in deciding whether or not to add or support a feature, and increase overall customer satisfaction.
