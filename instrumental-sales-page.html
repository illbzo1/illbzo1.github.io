Instrumental Sales Page

https://instrumentalapp.com/

copywriting, copy editing



This project was a rewrite of Instrumental's home page. The owners of Instrumental decided to shift Instrumental's positioning to appeal to larger tech companies that needed high-scale, highly customizable application and server monitoring.

I worked closely with the designer and marketing director, drawing upon real world implementations of Instrumental to clearly communicate the value of a customized application monitoring solution to an audience that is largely used to very rigid (and less customizable) monitoring tools.
