Instrumental Landing Page

https://instrumentalapp.com/application-monitoring

copywriting, keyword research

This is a landing page variation for an application monitoring campaign for Instrumental. The goal with this copy was to present Instrumental as an authority, selling the value of completely customized application monitoring. People who clicked through on an ad were automatically sent to one of two variations.

I worked with the designer to create two variations on landing pages for the application monitoring campaign. The goal of this campaign was not only to determine whether application monitoring itself was a good search term to target, but what tone resonated with our target audience.
