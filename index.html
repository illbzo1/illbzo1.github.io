My name is Tyler Moore.

I have a hard time with autobiographies. But lists are easy. I understand lists. I work well with a list.

Mid-thirties, Indianapolis, enthusiastic, a friend to cats, a fan of IPAs, a hugger, a high-fiver, an easy grinner, a loud laugher.
But most importantly:

I'm a writer

I've been writing since I was 8 years old. That nasty old saying "Writers like having written" is bullshit, as far as I'm concerned.

I'm happiest writing, alone in my home office, early in the morning or late at night, drinking black coffee or cold water, chewing through a story.

Writing is my Work. All caps, as a sign of respect. It demands respect, because it's dangerous, and it's fragile. Some days the Work is like putting a machine together. Some days it's like there's something alive in my gut, and it'll burn me alive if I don't get it out. Some days the Work is just work - it's boring, and it's flat, and it has to be done. But I love it, every second, every moment.

About this site

This is my personal site. I've had a few over the years - this is the latest thing. It's a place for me to mess around with CSS, and keep a super minimal blog about what I'm reading, what I'm writing, and anything that comes up along the way, and in between.
