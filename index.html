Hi! I'm Tyler, and I'm a full stack marketer. What does that mean? On one hand, it means I'm a generalist. On the other, it means I'm not tied to specific tools or practices. I take an adaptable, data-driven, and creative approach to growth and marketing. I work with businesses of all sizes to identify their pain points, come up with and implement possible solutions, and finally, measure the impact of the work I've done.

I'm highly curious, and a natural problem solver. I use both of these traits to help businesses improve their marketing. From customer development to funnel optimization to marketing automation - no two businesses will have the exact same problems, and a single tool set won't work in every case.

I'm also a fiction writer. I've been a fan of short, weird fiction since I could read. It's not much of a leap to go from "reader" to "writer", and I recently started writing my own short, weird fiction. I write mostly horror and speculative fiction, with some soft sci-fi thrown in for kicks.

I read a lot, play a ton of video games, and I like making people laugh. I'm an easy grinner, a high-fiver, a loud laugher, a friend to cats, and a fan of IPAs.
