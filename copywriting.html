Good copy is copy that works.

The first step to writing copy that works is to listen to the audience. Who is the product for? What problems do they have, and how do they talk about those problems? The target audience will tell me everything I need to write copy that resonates - from describing specific pain points, to the tone of the copy, to the medium for the message.

I write conversion-focused copy using research skills gained during my journalism school days and a lifelong love of writing. I like data-driven marketing, and I like measureable improvement.

But mostly I like solving problems. I like writing copy that helps people, and I like figuring out how to convince an audience to take action.
