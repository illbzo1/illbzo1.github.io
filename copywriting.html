Copywriting Samples

Here you'll find samples of my copywriting work. I've done a ton of conversion-focused writing for various media - from blog posts to banner ads, to social media to varations for a/b testing.

I'm especially interested in writing long-form copy. I love working on a sales page addressing a specific pain point, writing blog posts that provide clear, easy to understand instructions on how to solve a particularly difficult problem, and writing landing pages for paid advertising.

I love working on technical products, crafting copy to turn visitors into trialing users, and trialing users into happy, paying, long-term customers. I specialize in writing in-app messaging that gently guides a new signup along the funnel, and lifecycle emails that keep users happy, interested, and engaged with the product.
