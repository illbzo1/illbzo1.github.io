Codifferous Sales Page

https://codifferous.com/

copywriting, customer development, keyword research



Second version of copy for Codifferous's sales page. The first version was performing rather well, but the copy was based on assumptions the team felt were faulty. I spent a week talking to 40 developers who cared about code review, writing copy to position Codifferous as a product that would solve the biggest issues developers faced - code review takes up too much time, and it's too difficult.

The revised copy saw a big improvement in conversion and adoption rates, as well as long-term improvement in organic results for search terms related to code review.
