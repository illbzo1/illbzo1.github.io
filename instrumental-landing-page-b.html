Instrumental Landing Page

https://instrumentalapp.com/application-monitoring

copywriting, keyword research

This is a second variation of a landing page for a PPC campaign for Instrumental. As part of a bigger overhaul in Instrumental's marketing, I wanted to determine what sort of copy converted. With this take, I focused on questions Instrumental could answer based on a user's application data.

This copy talks about the problems a potential user will run into when they're operating an application. The goal was to convince visitors a customized monitoring solution was a better fit for their application, and we provide real life examples to show how Instrumental works.
