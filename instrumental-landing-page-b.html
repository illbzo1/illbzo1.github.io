Instrumental Landing Page

https://instrumentalapp.com/application-monitoring

copywriting, keyword research



This is another take on copy for a landing page for an application monitoring campaign. For this variation, I focused on the kinds of questions Instrumental could help answer, based on production data. Visitors to this page were evenly split between each variation.

For this campaign, I wanted to test whether a landing page positioning Instrumental as an authoritative voice in the space would converted better than a benefits-heavy landing page.
