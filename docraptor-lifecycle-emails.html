DocRaptor Lifecycle Emails

These are lifecycle emails sent to DocRaptor customers, at various points in their funnel.

This is a welcoming message sent to a new customer upon their first successful document creation request. The goal here is to make sure the customer is on the right path. Once a DocRaptor integration is working and successfully creating documents, the next step is to make sure the customer is getting the correct documents back.

Open rate: 60.9%
Response rate: 16.1%

This email is sent 24 hours after a trialing customer signs up, if they have not created a document. The goal is to encourage trialing users to finish their implementation, and to let them know DocRaptor support is ready and willing to help out every step of the way.

Open rate: 69.4%
Response rate: 16.7%

This email is sent immediately upon account cancellation. The goal of this email is to gather feedback from canceled users, identify areas for improvement, and manage long-term user churn.

Open rate: 65.2%
Response rate: 30.8%
