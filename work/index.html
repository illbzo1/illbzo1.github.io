Marketing is messaging.

At the most basic level, successful marketing is about getting your message to your audience. You might be surprised how many companies have messaging problems. I work with businesses of all sizes to identify their marketing challenges, come up with solutions, and help improve their bottom line. I love the energy of working with a startup, and I love the sheer amount of data available to an established brand.

My roots are in SEO, copywriting, and user experience, and over the years I've developed a broad skillset, honed from years of working with small companies with tight budgets. I always keep the end user in mind. What problems does the target audience face, and how can I reach them? What message will resonate?

Every business has unique challenges, and no two projects are the same. I enjoy the challenge of figuring out the best way to move a business forward - whether that's a messaging refresh, a deep analytics dive to identify opportunities for funnel optimization, or audience segmentation and a marketing automation campaign.
