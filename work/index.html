Marketing is messaging.

You might be surprised how many companies have messaging problems. I work with businesses of all sizes to identify their marketing challenges, come up with solutions, and help improve their bottom line. I love the energy of working with a startup, and I love the sheer amount of data available to an established brand.

Every business has unique challenges, and no two projects are the same. I enjoy the challenge of figuring out the best way to move a business forward - whether that's a messaging refresh, a deep analytics dive to identify opportunities for funnel optimization, or audience segmentation and a marketing automation campaign.

My roots are in SEO, copywriting, and user experience, but I've got a broad skillset, honed from years of working with small companies with tight budgets.
