Marketing is messaging.

Successful marketing is about communication - getting the right message to the right person at the right time. I work with businesses of all sizes to identify their marketing challenges, come up with solutions, and help improve their bottom line. I love the energy of working with a startup, and I love the sheer amount of data available to an established brand.

I've developed a broad marketing skillset, honed from years of working with small companies with tight budgets. I'm especially interested in marketing strategy, technical SEO, and user experience. What problems does the target audience face, and how can I reach them? What message will resonate?

Every business faces unique challenges, and no two projects are the same. I enjoy the challenge of figuring out the best way to move a business forward - whether that's a messaging refresh or a brand repositionin, a deep analytics dive to identify opportunities for funnel optimization, lead generation and lead generation, or a marketing automation campaign designed to help new users find success quickly.
